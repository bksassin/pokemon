About the Game

For Pokémon card enthusiasts and collectors.

Inspired by the classic 'Who's That Pokémon?' commercial break.

This game puts your knowledge of Pokémon cards to the test!

Compete against other trainers, climb the leaderboard, and become the ultimate Pokemon master.

Thanks to PACKADDX, you can enjoy this game for free and participate in giveaways for even more fun!
