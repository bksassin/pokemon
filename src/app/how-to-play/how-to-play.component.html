How to Play

Welcome, Trainer! Professor Oak here to guide you through this exciting Pokémon card guessing game. Are you ready to test your Pokémon knowledge? Let's begin!

Game Objective

Identify as many Pokémon as you can from their blurred card images to earn points and climb the trainer leaderboards.

Gameplay Instructions

  1. Observe the Card: A blurred Pokémon card will appear on your screen.
  2. Make Your Guess: Choose the correct Pokémon name from the given options.
  3. Score Points: Earn 1 point for each correct guess.
  4. Be Cautious: Two incorrect guesses on the same card will restart your game, so think carefully!
  5. Use Hints: Pay attention to the Pokémon type hint to narrow down your choices.
  6. Skip Challenging Cards: Earn 1 "Run" for every 50 points in your high score. Use these to skip difficult cards without penalty.
  7. Climb the Leaderboards: Compete against other trainers to see who can achieve the highest score!

Tips for Success

  • Study your Pokémon! Familiarize yourself with different Pokémon shapes and silhouettes.
  • Use the type hint wisely to eliminate unlikely options.
  • If you're unsure, it's sometimes better to use a "Run" than risk two wrong guesses.
  • Practice regularly to improve your recognition skills and reaction time.

Remember, the goal is to have fun while testing and expanding your Pokémon knowledge. Good luck, Trainer, and may the best Pokémon master win!
